About the site:

This website allows you to see the predicted outcome of Pokemon matchups.
See how a battle would play out based on type, level, moves, items etc.

To use the site:
Try inputing different Pokemon matchups of your liking in the text-boxes and try seeing how changing their level impacts the outcome of the battle.

Here are a couple examples:

  • Pikachu vs. Charizard
  • Bulbasaur vs. Squirtle
  • Tepig vs. Snivy
Once you've entered a valid matchup, click on the "battle" button to initiate a fight. Alternatively you can click the "random battle" button to start a battle between two random Pokemon.

Site settings:
By default, the battle text automatically scrolls just like in the official games but you can click the "turn on manual scroll" button to scroll through the text manually when clicking the "Next line" button.

Also, if you wish to see how the Pokedex works on the inside, you can click the "Stats for nerds" button to see the JSON data for the Pokemon currently in battle.

Note that some of the Pokemon in this database may be missing images or some info which could impact matchups. There are also no Gen 8 Pokemon (Sorry Sobble :0).

Lastly, feel free to rezise and zoom in your browser window for better results.
